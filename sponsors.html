Perk Details:

  • Networking Fair: Opportunity for sponsors to connect with and recruit Brown and RISD students as well as attending professionals. Participating sponsors will have access to resumes and portfolios of attending students. Also provides a platform to showcase innovative projects in Providence and beyond.

  • Content Presentation: Companies have the chance to send a representative to facilitate a workshop or give a lecture.

  • Goodie Bags: Collection of merchandise distributed to each attendee at the conference.

  • Snapchat Geofilter: Logo will be added to the BWxD snapchat geofilter for the duration of the conference. The geofilter will be viewed by anyone using Snapchat on College Hill.
